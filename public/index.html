FAQ

Common questions.

Is the pricing really per venue, not per user? ›
Yes. $19.95 AUD per venue per week, no matter how many staff you have in that venue. Add your whole cleaning crew, all your managers — it's the same price.
What happens after the 14-day free trial? ›
If you decide to subscribe, you'll be charged $19.95 per venue per week. If not, your account goes into read-only mode — you can still view your data but can't add tasks, raise issues, or invite team members. No charge is made without your action.
Can I add or remove venues after I subscribe? ›
Yes. Add a venue in the app and your subscription is updated automatically — you're charged the prorated difference for the remaining days in the week. Remove a venue and the same applies in reverse.
Which devices does the app run on? ›
Venues V is a native iOS and Android app. Owners also have access to a web dashboard at client.venuesv.com. Your team can use any device — it all syncs in real time.
Do cleaners need their own account? ›
Yes. Each team member gets their own login so there's individual accountability. You invite them from the Team screen — they get an email with their credentials. No cost per user.
Is my data secure? ›
Yes. Venues V uses Supabase with Row Level Security for strict multi-tenant data isolation — your data is separated from other customers. Payments are handled securely by Stripe.
Can I cancel anytime? ›
Yes, no lock-in. Cancel anytime from your subscription settings. You keep access until the end of your current billing week.
