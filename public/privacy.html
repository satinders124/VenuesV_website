Privacy Policy

Last updated: 12 July 2026

Venues V ("we", "us", "our") operates the Venues V mobile application and website (venuesv.com). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our platform.

By using Venues V, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

Account information

When you sign up, we collect your name, email address, phone number, and password. This information is used to create and manage your account.

Venue and operations data

We collect data you enter into the platform, including venue names, zones, tasks, issues, photos, and team member details. This is the core operational data of the service.

Usage data

We collect information about how you use the app, including login times, feature usage, and device information (device type, OS version, app version).

Payment information

Payments are processed by Stripe. We do not store your credit card number, expiry, or CVC. We retain your Stripe customer ID and subscription status to manage your account.

Photos

Photos uploaded to issues and tasks are stored in Supabase Storage. Access is restricted to authorised users for the relevant venue.

Push notification tokens

We store your device push token to send you notifications. You can disable notifications at any time in your device settings.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the Venues V platform
  • To manage your account and subscription
  • To send transactional emails (account creation, password reset, billing)
  • To send push notifications for issues, tasks, and messages
  • To send product updates if you opted in during signup
  • To investigate and resolve support requests
  • To improve the platform

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We do not share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

We share data only with trusted service providers necessary to operate the platform:

  • Supabase — database, authentication, storage, and real-time services
  • Vercel — website hosting and secure server-side billing functions
  • Stripe — payment processing and subscription management
  • Our configured email delivery provider — account confirmation and transactional email delivery
  • Expo / Expo Push — mobile push notification delivery

All service providers are bound by data processing agreements and are prohibited from using your data for any other purpose.

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid legal requests by public authorities.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • All data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS
  • Data at rest is encrypted by our cloud infrastructure providers
  • Supabase Row Level Security (RLS) enforces multi-tenant isolation — your data is only accessible to authorised users in your account
  • Payment data is handled entirely by Stripe and never touches our servers
  • Admin access to production data is restricted and logged

No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, but we take reasonable precautions to protect your information.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. Resolved issues are automatically deleted after 7 days. If you cancel your account, your data is retained for 30 days before deletion, to allow for account recovery.

You may request deletion of your data at any time by emailing hello@venuesv.com.

6. Your Rights

Under Australian Privacy Principles (APPs) and applicable law, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC)

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@venuesv.com.

7. Cookies

The Venues V website uses minimal cookies for session management and analytics. We do not use third-party advertising cookies. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect functionality.

8. Children's Privacy

Venues V is designed for business use and is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by email or by posting a notice in the app. Your continued use of the platform after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: hello@venuesv.com
  • Website: venuesv.com
